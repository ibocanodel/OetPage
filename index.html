Get Started

Studying English never became so easy!!

Online Education Tool (OET) aims to create virtual classrooms where instructors and student collaborate. Students can solve assignments according to their level and instructors can assign new assignments to the classrooms and track their students progress.

Now, the classrooms are just one click away.

User-friendly interface

Assignment pages have many tools to help student's learning experience.

Level profiling

Students will face assignments according to their level.

Feedback System

Students will get feedbacks from their instructor.

Tracking of student progress

Instructors can see the student's assignments and averages with colorful graphs.

The center of our system is of course students who want to improve their English.

We offer them an environment where they can exercise and learn in an efficient and fun way.

  • They can solve a wide variety of questions without worry of losing their answers. The system will automatically save all the answers, so they can continue where they left even if accidentally exiting.
  • Apart from assignments specific to their class, students will have an option to see questions of their own difficulty level starting from easy. They will be able to level up while getting more and more correct results.
  • They can choose articles to read based on their interested categories. Besides, they can highlight parts they want and take their own online notes for each assignment.
  • They can reach their past assignments and grades together with the histogram of their class. Students who are eager to see performance of themselves can view how much time they spent on each assignment.
  • They will be notified when a new assignment is posted, or the instructor gives a feedback.
  • In order to improve their pronunciation, we support Text-to-Speech feature for all the reading assignments. Thus, they can hear the text by a native speaker.

The most important role for such an education tool; instructors.

Instructors are responsible for their classrooms. They will be assigned to existing classrooms by the admins. Some of their capabilities are listed below,

  • They can create new assignments with lots of different types of questions. Also, they can preview an assignment to see how it will look like in student interface before submitting.
  • They can see existing assignments created by other instructors, and they can use these assignments for their classrooms.
  • They can review past assignments of students. Also, they can grade written assignments and give feedbacks to their students.
  • They can see general performances of the students.

The administrators are the people who manage the entire system.

The admins are responsible for management of the system. Their capabilities are listed below;

  • They can create new accounts for students and instructors.
  • They can create new classrooms by specifying some attributes such as capacity, english level, etc.
  • They can add students/instructors to existing classrooms.
  • They can list existing accounts/classrooms. Also, they can update some attributes of accounts and classrooms.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What kind of questions can be used in assignments?

    6 different types of questions can be used; multiple choice, true/false, matching, gap-filling, ordering and written questions. While written questions are graded by instructors, other types of questions are graded automatically when the student submits answer form.

  • Can students and instructors communicate?

    Instructors and students can communicate easily through feedbacks and messages.

  • Is it free?

    OET is a free tool that aims to provide English education to every student.

  • What is your app's difference from other online education platforms?

    OET is a more flexible and adaptable application compared to others in the market. It is open to further development in collaboration with future clients.

  • Is it available as a mobile application?

    Not yet! But, it is possible in the future.

  • How can I register to the system as a student or instructor?

    You cannot enroll yourself to the system, please communicate with your administration staff.
